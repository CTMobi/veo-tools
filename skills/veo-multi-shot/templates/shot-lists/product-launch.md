# Product Launch Shot List

A 4-shot narrative arc designed for product reveals, feature announcements, and launch campaigns.

**Total Duration**: ~26 seconds
**Estimated Cost**: ~$2.00

---

## Narrative Arc

```
TEASER → REVEAL → DETAIL → CONTEXT
```

| Beat | Purpose | Emotional Goal |
|------|---------|----------------|
| Teaser | Build anticipation | Curiosity, intrigue |
| Reveal | First glimpse of product | Excitement, "wow" moment |
| Detail | Showcase craftsmanship | Appreciation, desire |
| Context | Product in its world | Aspiration, connection |

---

## Shot Specifications

### Shot 1: TEASER (8s)

**Purpose**: Build anticipation without revealing the product directly.

| Attribute | Specification |
|-----------|---------------|
| Beat | Teaser |
| Duration | 8 seconds |
| Shot Size | Wide or Medium-Wide |
| Camera | Slow dolly forward or slow push |
| Subject | Environment, abstract forms, or suggestion of product |
| Action | Approaching, emerging, building |

**Emotional Journey**: Mystery → Curiosity → Anticipation

**Prompt Elements**:
- Lead with camera movement toward something
- Use atmospheric elements (particles, light, depth)
- Suggest without revealing
- Build tension through pacing

**Example Prompt Structure**:
```
Slow dolly forward through [environment], [atmospheric elements],
[Visual DNA: palette], [Visual DNA: lighting], [Visual DNA: atmosphere],
mysterious anticipation building, [abstract/suggested form in distance]
```

---

### Shot 2: REVEAL (6s)

**Purpose**: First clear view of the product—the money shot.

| Attribute | Specification |
|-----------|---------------|
| Beat | Reveal |
| Duration | 6 seconds |
| Shot Size | Medium |
| Camera | Push in, pull out, or smooth orbit begin |
| Subject | Product in hero position |
| Action | Emerging, illuminating, materializing |

**Emotional Journey**: Anticipation → Recognition → Excitement

**Prompt Elements**:
- Center the product clearly
- Light reveals form (emerging from shadow or spotlight)
- Clean, uncluttered composition
- Moment of arrival

**Example Prompt Structure**:
```
Smooth push in toward [product description], [emerging action],
[Visual DNA: palette], [Visual DNA: lighting], [Visual DNA: atmosphere],
first reveal of [product], moment of recognition
```

---

### Shot 3: DETAIL (6s)

**Purpose**: Showcase material quality, craftsmanship, and detail.

| Attribute | Specification |
|-----------|---------------|
| Beat | Detail |
| Duration | 6 seconds |
| Shot Size | Close-up or Macro |
| Camera | Slow orbit, subtle dolly, or static with moving light |
| Subject | Product detail, material, or signature feature |
| Action | Examining, highlighting, revealing texture |

**Emotional Journey**: Curiosity → Appreciation → Desire

**Prompt Elements**:
- Extreme material specificity
- Highlight textures and surfaces
- Single hero detail (not everything at once)
- Premium lighting that reveals quality

**Example Prompt Structure**:
```
[Slow orbit/macro lens], intimate view of [specific detail with material],
light traveling across surface revealing [texture/finish],
[Visual DNA: palette], [Visual DNA: lighting], [Visual DNA: atmosphere],
premium craftsmanship
```

---

### Shot 4: CONTEXT (6s)

**Purpose**: Show the product in its intended world—the emotional payoff.

| Attribute | Specification |
|-----------|---------------|
| Beat | Context |
| Duration | 6 seconds |
| Shot Size | Wide or Medium-Wide |
| Camera | Pull out, crane up, or establishing static |
| Subject | Product in environment or use scenario |
| Action | Settling, integrating, belonging |

**Emotional Journey**: Understanding → Aspiration → Connection

**Prompt Elements**:
- Product as part of a larger story
- Environment that communicates lifestyle/value
- Sense of completion or arrival
- Emotional landing

**Example Prompt Structure**:
```
Smooth pull out revealing [product] within [aspirational environment],
[contextual elements that communicate value], settling into its place,
[Visual DNA: palette], [Visual DNA: lighting], [Visual DNA: atmosphere],
[emotional resolution descriptor]
```

## Complete Example: Tech Product Launch

**Product**: Premium wireless headphones
**Visual DNA**: tech-saas (cool blues, soft diffused light, ethereal tech atmosphere)

### Shot Table

| # | Beat | Duration | Camera | Subject | Key Moment |
|---|------|----------|--------|---------|------------|
| 1 | Teaser | 8s | Slow dolly forward | Abstract tech environment | Approaching mystery |
| 2 | Reveal | 6s | Push in | Headphones emerging from shadow | First glimpse |
| 3 | Detail | 6s | Slow orbit | Ear cup material, controls | Craftsmanship |
| 4 | Context | 6s | Pull out | Headphones on modern desk | Lifestyle integration |

### Full Prompts

**Shot 1 - Teaser**:
```
Slow dolly forward through minimal tech environment with floating geometric forms,
soft particles drifting through cool blue ambient light with subtle warm accents,
soft diffused lighting with rim separation, ethereal premium tech atmosphere,
mysterious silhouette suggesting product form in distance, anticipation building
```

**Shot 2 - Reveal**:
```
Smooth push in toward premium wireless headphones emerging from soft shadow,
form gradually illuminating, cool blue palette with warm amber accent highlights,
soft diffused lighting with subtle rim separation, ethereal tech atmosphere,
first reveal, moment of recognition
```

**Shot 3 - Detail**:
```
Slow orbit, macro lens, intimate view of brushed aluminum ear cup surface,
light traveling across revealing microscopic texture and premium finish,
cool blue reflections with warm amber accent, soft diffused key light,
ethereal tech atmosphere, premium craftsmanship appreciation
```

**Shot 4 - Context**:
```
Smooth pull out revealing headphones resting on modern minimal desk,
soft morning light through window, plant shadow in background,
cool blue palette with warm ambient accents, soft diffused natural light,
ethereal tech atmosphere, settled into its place, aspirational lifestyle moment
```

## Customization Guide

### Adjusting Pacing

| Feeling | Modification |
|---------|--------------|
| More dramatic | Extend Teaser to 8s, use slow motion descriptors |
| More energetic | Shorten Teaser to 6s, increase camera movement speed |
| More premium | Add pause/stillness moments in prompts |

### Alternative Camera Progressions

| Style | Shot 1 | Shot 2 | Shot 3 | Shot 4 |
|-------|--------|--------|--------|--------|
| Default | Dolly forward | Push in | Orbit | Pull out |
| Contemplative | Static | Slow push | Static + light move | Crane up |
| Dynamic | Tracking | Push in | Fast orbit | Sweeping pull |

### Duration Variations

| Total Target | Shot Durations |
|--------------|----------------|
| ~22s (shorter) | 6, 6, 4, 6 |
| ~26s (default) | 8, 6, 6, 6 |
| ~30s (extended) | 8, 8, 6, 8 |

---

## Transitions

**Recommended**: Crossfade (0.5s)
- Smooth flow between beats
- Maintains premium feel
- Works with varied pacing

**Alternative**: Cut
- Use for high-energy products
- Creates punchy, dynamic feel
- Best with faster camera movements
