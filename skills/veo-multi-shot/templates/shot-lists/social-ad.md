# Social Ad Shot List

A 3-shot narrative arc optimized for social media advertising (Instagram, TikTok, YouTube pre-roll).

**Total Duration**: ~18 seconds
**Estimated Cost**: ~$1.50

---

## Narrative Arc

```
HOOK → MESSAGE → CTA SETUP
```

| Beat | Purpose | Emotional Goal |
|------|---------|----------------|
| Hook | Stop the scroll, grab attention | Intrigue, "what is this?" |
| Message | Deliver the value proposition | Understanding, desire |
| CTA Setup | Create momentum toward action | Urgency, motivation |

---

## Design Principles for Social

### The 3-Second Rule
Social users decide in ~3 seconds whether to keep watching. Shot 1 must:
- Start with motion or visual interest (no slow builds)
- Have strong visual contrast
- Create immediate curiosity

### Sound-Off Optimization
Many social views happen without sound. Ensure:
- Visual story is clear without audio
- Key moments are visually distinct
- Motion communicates energy and mood

### Vertical Consideration
Consider 9:16 aspect ratio for mobile-first platforms:
- Taller framing means more vertical subjects
- Less horizontal space for environment
- Face and product-centric framing works better

## Shot Specifications

### Shot 1: HOOK (6s)

**Purpose**: Stop the scroll. Immediate visual interest that creates curiosity.

| Attribute | Specification |
|-----------|---------------|
| Beat | Hook |
| Duration | 6 seconds |
| Shot Size | Variable (whatever's most striking) |
| Camera | Dynamic—movement from frame 1 |
| Subject | Most visually arresting element |
| Action | Immediate, continuous motion |

**Emotional Journey**: Attention → Curiosity → "Tell me more"

**Critical Rules**:
- **NO SLOW BUILD** — Start with motion, not stillness
- First frame must be visually interesting
- Create question in viewer's mind

**Prompt Elements**:
- Lead with dynamic camera or subject movement
- High visual contrast
- Unusual angle or perspective
- Something unexpected or beautiful

**Example Prompt Structure**:
```
[Dynamic camera movement], [striking visual from frame 1],
[immediate motion/action], [high contrast element],
[Visual DNA: palette], [Visual DNA: lighting], [Visual DNA: atmosphere],
attention-grabbing, [curiosity-inducing element]
```

---

### Shot 2: MESSAGE (6s)

**Purpose**: Deliver the core value proposition clearly and memorably.

| Attribute | Specification |
|-----------|---------------|
| Beat | Message |
| Duration | 6 seconds |
| Shot Size | Medium (product/service clearly visible) |
| Camera | Controlled—focus on clarity |
| Subject | Product, service, or benefit in action |
| Action | Demonstrating, revealing, functioning |

**Emotional Journey**: Understanding → Desire → "I want that"

**Critical Rules**:
- Product/benefit must be unmistakably clear
- Show, don't tell (no text reliance)
- Maintain energy from hook

**Prompt Elements**:
- Clear product/benefit visibility
- Action that demonstrates value
- Maintained visual energy
- Aspiration or desire trigger

**Example Prompt Structure**:
```
[Clear camera angle], [product/benefit clearly visible],
[action demonstrating value], [aspirational context],
[Visual DNA: palette], [Visual DNA: lighting], [Visual DNA: atmosphere],
[value proposition visual metaphor]
```

---

### Shot 3: CTA SETUP (6s)

**Purpose**: Create forward momentum. Set up the call-to-action that follows.

| Attribute | Specification |
|-----------|---------------|
| Beat | CTA Setup |
| Duration | 6 seconds |
| Shot Size | Medium or Wide |
| Camera | Resolving but leaving energy |
| Subject | Product in context or completion moment |
| Action | Completing, arriving, inviting |

**Emotional Journey**: Completion → Motivation → "I should act"

**Critical Rules**:
- Don't fully resolve—leave tension for CTA
- Maintain forward energy
- End on aspiration, not conclusion

**Prompt Elements**:
- Product positioned for overlay text (space for CTA)
- Energy pointing forward
- Aspirational framing
- Clean area for text overlay

**Example Prompt Structure**:
```
[Camera movement toward resolution], [product in aspirational context],
[forward-leaning energy], [clean compositional space for CTA],
[Visual DNA: palette], [Visual DNA: lighting], [Visual DNA: atmosphere],
momentum toward action, [aspiration trigger]
```

## Complete Example: Fitness App Social Ad

**Product**: Fitness tracking app
**Visual DNA**: Custom (energetic teal and coral, dynamic lighting, kinetic atmosphere)
**Aspect Ratio**: 9:16 (vertical for Stories/Reels)

### Shot Table

| # | Beat | Duration | Camera | Subject | Key Moment |
|---|------|----------|--------|---------|------------|
| 1 | Hook | 6s | Fast push in | Abstract data visualization | Energy burst |
| 2 | Message | 6s | Tracking | Fitness metrics flowing | Value clear |
| 3 | CTA Setup | 6s | Pull to center | App interface glow | Ready for CTA |

### Full Prompts

**Shot 1 - Hook**:
```
Fast push in through burst of abstract fitness data particles,
teal and coral energy streams flowing dynamically from frame one,
high contrast lighting with vibrant color, kinetic tech atmosphere,
immediate motion, arresting visual energy, curiosity trigger
```

**Shot 2 - Message**:
```
Smooth tracking following flowing fitness metrics visualization,
abstract heart rate, steps, and achievement icons flowing cohesively,
teal and coral palette with dynamic lighting accents,
kinetic atmosphere, value proposition clear, forward momentum
```

**Shot 3 - CTA Setup**:
```
Pull back to centered composition, abstract app interface glowing,
metrics converging into unified form, energy maintained but directing,
teal and coral with focused lighting, kinetic but resolving,
clean space in composition for overlay, momentum toward action
```

## Platform-Specific Variations

### Instagram Reels / TikTok (9:16)

| Consideration | Adjustment |
|---------------|------------|
| Aspect Ratio | 9:16 vertical |
| Pacing | Can be slightly faster |
| Framing | Vertical subjects, face-centric |
| Safe Zones | Account for UI overlay areas |

### YouTube Pre-Roll (16:9)

| Consideration | Adjustment |
|---------------|------------|
| Aspect Ratio | 16:9 horizontal |
| Skip Button | First 5s must hook before skip available |
| Pacing | Hook must be even faster |
| Branding | Can appear earlier |

### LinkedIn / Twitter (various)

| Consideration | Adjustment |
|---------------|------------|
| Aspect Ratio | 16:9 or 1:1 |
| Pacing | Can be more measured |
| Tone | More professional |
| Messaging | Value-focused over emotion |

## Customization Guide

### Adjusting Energy Level

| Energy | Modifications |
|--------|---------------|
| High energy | Faster camera, more dynamic movement, higher contrast |
| Moderate | Smooth continuous motion, balanced contrast |
| Premium/calm | Slower motion, softer contrast, more space |

### Alternative Hook Strategies

| Strategy | Prompt Approach |
|----------|-----------------|
| Question | Unusual perspective that makes viewer wonder |
| Beauty | Stunning visual that demands attention |
| Motion | Dynamic movement from first frame |
| Contrast | Unexpected juxtaposition |
| Scale | Macro or extreme wide for surprise |

### Duration Variations

| Total Target | Shot Durations | Platform |
|--------------|----------------|----------|
| ~15s (short) | 4, 6, 5 | TikTok, Reels |
| ~18s (default) | 6, 6, 6 | General social |
| ~20s (extended) | 6, 8, 6 | YouTube, LinkedIn |

---

## Transitions

**Recommended**: Cut or fast crossfade (0.25s)
- Maintains energy and pace
- Feels native to social content
- No wasted frames

**Alternative**: Whip/blur transition
- Adds energy between shots
- Matches high-energy content
- Use sparingly

---

## Post-Production Notes

Social ads typically need:
- **Text overlay** for CTA (add in post)
- **Audio track** (separate from Veo generation)
- **End card** with logo/CTA (add in post)
- **Captions** for accessibility

Generate clips without audio (`--audio off`), add music and VO in post-production for better control.
