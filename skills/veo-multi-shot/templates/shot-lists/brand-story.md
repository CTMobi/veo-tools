# Brand Story Shot List

A 5-shot narrative arc designed for brand films, company overviews, and emotional storytelling.

**Total Duration**: ~34 seconds
**Estimated Cost**: ~$2.50

---

## Narrative Arc

```
ESTABLISHING → JOURNEY → DISCOVERY → CONNECTION → RESOLUTION
```

| Beat | Purpose | Emotional Goal |
|------|---------|----------------|
| Establishing | Set the world and tone | Orientation, atmosphere |
| Journey | Show movement and progress | Engagement, momentum |
| Discovery | Reveal the insight or value | Understanding, "aha" |
| Connection | Human element or relationship | Emotion, identification |
| Resolution | Landing and payoff | Satisfaction, aspiration |

---

## Shot Specifications

### Shot 1: ESTABLISHING (8s)

**Purpose**: Immerse the viewer in the brand's world and establish tone.

| Attribute | Specification |
|-----------|---------------|
| Beat | Establishing |
| Duration | 8 seconds |
| Shot Size | Wide |
| Camera | Slow dolly, crane, or static atmospheric |
| Subject | Environment that embodies brand values |
| Action | Observing, breathing, existing |

**Emotional Journey**: Arrival → Orientation → Immersion

**Prompt Elements**:
- Wide environmental context
- Strong atmosphere and mood
- Time and place anchors
- Visual metaphor for brand values

**Example Prompt Structure**:
```
[Wide camera movement] through [brand-aligned environment],
[atmospheric elements suggesting values], [temporal anchor],
[Visual DNA: palette], [Visual DNA: lighting], [Visual DNA: atmosphere],
establishing the world, [brand emotion adjective]
```

---

### Shot 2: JOURNEY (6s)

**Purpose**: Create sense of movement, progress, and forward momentum.

| Attribute | Specification |
|-----------|---------------|
| Beat | Journey |
| Duration | 6 seconds |
| Shot Size | Medium or tracking |
| Camera | Tracking, dolly, or continuous movement |
| Subject | Motion, process, or transition |
| Action | Moving, progressing, flowing |

**Emotional Journey**: Movement → Progress → Anticipation

**Prompt Elements**:
- Sense of forward motion
- Visual metaphor for progress/growth
- Continuous, flowing movement
- Building energy

**Example Prompt Structure**:
```
[Tracking/continuous movement] following [movement metaphor],
sense of progress and momentum, [process or flow elements],
[Visual DNA: palette], [Visual DNA: lighting], [Visual DNA: atmosphere],
journey forward, [progress descriptor]
```

---

### Shot 3: DISCOVERY (6s)

**Purpose**: The "aha" moment—reveal the insight, innovation, or value proposition.

| Attribute | Specification |
|-----------|---------------|
| Beat | Discovery |
| Duration | 6 seconds |
| Shot Size | Medium or Close-up |
| Camera | Push in, orbit reveal, or focus shift |
| Subject | The key insight, product, or moment |
| Action | Revealing, illuminating, understanding |

**Emotional Journey**: Curiosity → Revelation → Understanding

**Prompt Elements**:
- Clear focal point of discovery
- Moment of clarity or revelation
- Light as metaphor for understanding
- Emphasis on the "thing" being discovered

**Example Prompt Structure**:
```
[Revealing camera movement] as [discovery subject] comes into focus,
moment of clarity, [illumination or revelation element],
[Visual DNA: palette], [Visual DNA: lighting], [Visual DNA: atmosphere],
the insight revealed, [understanding emotion]
```

---

### Shot 4: CONNECTION (6s)

**Purpose**: Emotional resonance—the human element or relationship to viewer.

| Attribute | Specification |
|-----------|---------------|
| Beat | Connection |
| Duration | 6 seconds |
| Shot Size | Medium or Close-up |
| Camera | Intimate: push, static, or gentle movement |
| Subject | Human element, relatable moment, or emotional detail |
| Action | Connecting, relating, feeling |

**Emotional Journey**: Observation → Recognition → Identification

**Prompt Elements**:
- Human scale or relatable element
- Emotional warmth or resonance
- Intimacy and closeness
- Moment that viewers see themselves in

**Example Prompt Structure**:
```
[Intimate camera movement], [human-scale or relatable subject],
moment of connection, [emotional detail],
[Visual DNA: palette], [Visual DNA: lighting], [Visual DNA: atmosphere],
[warmth/emotion descriptor], feeling of identification
```

---

### Shot 5: RESOLUTION (8s)

**Purpose**: Satisfying conclusion—where the story lands and what it means.

| Attribute | Specification |
|-----------|---------------|
| Beat | Resolution |
| Duration | 8 seconds |
| Shot Size | Wide or Medium-Wide |
| Camera | Pull out, crane up, or settling movement |
| Subject | Complete picture, synthesis, or arrival |
| Action | Resolving, completing, arriving |

**Emotional Journey**: Completion → Satisfaction → Aspiration

**Prompt Elements**:
- Sense of completeness
- Elevated or aspirational framing
- Visual synthesis of themes
- Emotional landing and release

**Example Prompt Structure**:
```
[Expansive camera movement] revealing [complete scene or synthesis],
sense of arrival and completion, [aspirational elements],
[Visual DNA: palette], [Visual DNA: lighting], [Visual DNA: atmosphere],
resolution, [satisfaction/aspiration emotion]
```

## Complete Example: Sustainable Brand Story

**Brand**: Eco-friendly outdoor apparel company
**Visual DNA**: Custom (earth tones, golden natural light, organic contemplative atmosphere)

### Shot Table

| # | Beat | Duration | Camera | Subject | Key Moment |
|---|------|----------|--------|---------|------------|
| 1 | Establishing | 8s | Slow crane up | Mountain landscape at dawn | The wild world |
| 2 | Journey | 6s | Tracking forward | Trail through forest | Moving forward |
| 3 | Discovery | 6s | Push in | Sustainable material detail | The innovation |
| 4 | Connection | 6s | Static intimate | Hands touching fabric | Human touch |
| 5 | Resolution | 8s | Crane up/pull out | Person overlooking vista | Belonging |

### Full Prompts

**Shot 1 - Establishing**:
```
Slow crane up revealing vast mountain landscape at first light,
morning mist in valleys, golden dawn light breaking over peaks,
earth tones with soft greens and warm amber highlights,
natural golden hour lighting, soft atmospheric haze,
organic contemplative atmosphere, the wild world awaits
```

**Shot 2 - Journey**:
```
Tracking forward along forest trail, dappled light through canopy,
ferns and undergrowth flowing past, sense of forward movement,
earth tones with deep forest greens, natural filtered light,
organic contemplative atmosphere, journey into nature
```

**Shot 3 - Discovery**:
```
Smooth push in toward close-up of sustainable fabric texture,
woven fibers visible, light revealing natural material quality,
earth tones with warm amber accents, soft diffused light,
organic atmosphere, moment of understanding, innovation in craft
```

**Shot 4 - Connection**:
```
Static camera, intimate view of hands touching fabric surface,
gentle motion, human scale and warmth, tactile connection,
earth tones with warm skin tones, soft natural light,
organic contemplative atmosphere, human connection to material
```

**Shot 5 - Resolution**:
```
Slow crane up from person standing at overlook, revealing vast vista,
golden hour light flooding scene, sense of belonging and possibility,
earth tones with amber and gold highlights, dramatic natural light,
organic contemplative atmosphere, arrived, at home in the world
```

## Customization Guide

### Adjusting Emotional Tone

| Tone | Modifications |
|------|---------------|
| More inspiring | Increase light intensity, add upward camera movements |
| More intimate | Use closer shots, slower movements, warmer light |
| More dramatic | Add contrast, use harder light, more dynamic camera |
| More serene | Slower everything, softer light, minimal movement |

### Alternative Narrative Structures

| Variation | Arc | When to Use |
|-----------|-----|-------------|
| Challenge-focused | Problem → Struggle → Insight → Solution → Triumph | Problem-solving brands |
| Origin story | Past → Evolution → Breakthrough → Present → Future | Heritage brands |
| Transformation | Before → Catalyst → Change → Growth → After | Personal development |

### Duration Variations

| Total Target | Shot Durations |
|--------------|----------------|
| ~30s (shorter) | 6, 6, 6, 6, 6 |
| ~34s (default) | 8, 6, 6, 6, 8 |
| ~40s (extended) | 8, 8, 8, 8, 8 |

---

## Transitions

**Recommended**: Crossfade (0.5-0.75s)
- Dreamy, narrative flow
- Emphasizes journey and connection
- Matches contemplative pacing

**Alternative**: Fade through black (0.5s)
- Use between major emotional shifts
- Creates chapter-like feeling
- Good for more dramatic stories
