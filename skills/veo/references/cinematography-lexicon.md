# Cinematography Lexicon

A vocabulary reference for constructing precise Veo prompts. Use professional film terminology to communicate intent clearly.

---

## Camera Movements

### Dolly
Camera physically moves toward or away from subject on tracks/wheels.

| Term | Motion | Best For |
|------|--------|----------|
| `dolly in` / `dolly forward` | Toward subject | Building intimacy, drawing viewer in |
| `dolly out` / `dolly back` | Away from subject | Revealing context, creating distance |
| `dolly alongside` | Parallel to moving subject | Following action |

**Veo prompt pattern**: `Slow dolly forward through [environment]...`

### Tracking / Truck
Camera moves laterally, parallel to action.

| Term | Motion | Best For |
|------|--------|----------|
| `tracking left/right` | Lateral movement | Revealing space progressively |
| `truck shot` | Same as tracking | Alternative terminology |

**Veo prompt pattern**: `Smooth tracking shot moving left past [subjects]...`

### Crane / Jib
Camera moves vertically or in sweeping arcs.

| Term | Motion | Best For |
|------|--------|----------|
| `crane up` | Rising vertical | Revealing scope, establishing |
| `crane down` | Descending vertical | Landing on subject |
| `jib arm` | Smaller arc movements | Fluid transitions |

**Veo prompt pattern**: `Crane shot rising above [environment], revealing [vista]...`

### Orbit
Camera circles around a central subject.

| Term | Motion | Best For |
|------|--------|----------|
| `orbit` / `arc` | Circular around subject | Product showcase, 360 view |
| `half orbit` | 180 degrees | Perspective shift |
| `slow orbit` | Gentle circular | Contemplative examination |

**Veo prompt pattern**: `Slow orbit around [subject], completing quarter rotation...`

### Push / Pull
Directional emphasis without dolly terminology.

| Term | Motion | Best For |
|------|--------|----------|
| `push in` | Moving toward, intensifying | Building tension |
| `pull out` | Moving away, expanding | Revealing twist, context |

**Veo prompt pattern**: `Slow push in on [subject detail]...`

### Pan / Tilt
Camera rotates on fixed position.

| Term | Motion | Best For |
|------|--------|----------|
| `pan left/right` | Horizontal rotation | Surveying scene |
| `tilt up/down` | Vertical rotation | Revealing height |
| `whip pan` | Rapid horizontal | Transition, energy |

**Veo prompt pattern**: `Slow pan across [environment], settling on [subject]...`

### Static / Locked
No camera movement.

| Term | Motion | Best For |
|------|--------|----------|
| `static camera` | Fixed position | Observation, loops |
| `locked camera` | Tripod-stable | Hero backgrounds |
| `fixed frame` | Composition-focused | Tableau |

**Veo prompt pattern**: `Locked camera observing [subject with subtle motion]...`

## Lens Terminology

### Focal Length Character

| Lens Type | Visual Effect | Best For |
|-----------|--------------|----------|
| `wide angle` / `wide lens` | Expanded space, slight distortion | Environment, scale |
| `normal lens` | Natural perspective | General use |
| `telephoto` | Compressed planes, shallow DOF | Isolation, intimacy |
| `macro lens` | Extreme close-up, very shallow DOF | Detail, texture |

### Depth of Field

| Term | Effect | Best For |
|------|--------|----------|
| `shallow depth of field` | Subject sharp, background blurred | Focus attention |
| `deep focus` | Everything sharp | Environmental storytelling |
| `bokeh` | Beautiful blur quality | Aesthetic background |
| `rack focus` | Focus shifts between planes | Redirecting attention |

**Veo prompt pattern**: `Macro lens, extreme shallow depth of field, [subject detail]...`

## Lighting Vocabulary

### Direction

| Term | Position | Character |
|------|----------|-----------|
| `front light` | From camera position | Flat, even |
| `side light` | 90 degrees to camera | Texture, dimension |
| `back light` / `rim light` | Behind subject | Separation, silhouette |
| `top light` | Directly above | Drama, mystery |
| `under light` | From below | Unnatural, sinister |

### Quality

| Term | Character | Best For |
|------|-----------|----------|
| `hard light` | Sharp shadows, defined edges | Drama, high contrast |
| `soft light` | Diffused, gentle gradients | Flattering, natural |
| `diffused light` | Even, scattered | Overcast, studio |
| `specular` | Bright highlights | Metallic, wet surfaces |

### Motivation

| Term | Meaning | Best For |
|------|---------|----------|
| `motivated lighting` | Light appears to come from visible source | Realism |
| `practical lights` | In-frame light sources | Atmosphere |
| `natural light` | Sun, sky, window | Organic feel |
| `neon light` | Artificial colored sources | Urban, night |

### Time of Day

| Term | Character | Color Temperature |
|------|-----------|-------------------|
| `golden hour` | Warm, long shadows | Orange/amber |
| `blue hour` | Cool, twilight | Deep blue |
| `magic hour` | Transitional, mixed | Warm to cool gradient |
| `high noon` | Harsh, overhead | Neutral white |
| `overcast` | Soft, directionless | Cool, flat |

**Veo prompt pattern**: `...golden hour light streaming through [element], long shadows...`

## Frame and Composition

### Shot Sizes

| Term | Framing | Best For |
|------|---------|----------|
| `extreme wide` / `EWS` | Environment dominates | Establishing, scale |
| `wide shot` / `WS` | Full subject in environment | Context |
| `medium shot` / `MS` | Subject waist-up | Dialogue, action |
| `close-up` / `CU` | Face or detail fills frame | Emotion, detail |
| `extreme close-up` / `ECU` | Feature fills frame | Intensity, texture |

### Composition Techniques

| Term | Description | Best For |
|------|-------------|----------|
| `rule of thirds` | Subject at grid intersections | Balance |
| `center frame` | Subject dead center | Symmetry, power |
| `negative space` | Deliberate emptiness | Isolation, breathing room |
| `leading lines` | Lines draw eye to subject | Direction |
| `frame within frame` | Element creates sub-frame | Depth, focus |

## Motion Speed

| Term | Speed | Best For |
|------|-------|----------|
| `slow motion` | Below real-time | Drama, beauty, detail |
| `real-time` | 1:1 playback | Natural action |
| `time-lapse` | Compressed time | Clouds, crowds, growth |
| `hyperlapse` | Time-lapse with movement | Journey, transformation |

**Veo prompt pattern**: `...slow motion capture of [action]...`

## Atmospheric Elements

### Particles and Haze

| Term | Visual | Best For |
|------|--------|----------|
| `dust motes` | Tiny particles in light beams | Age, atmosphere |
| `fog` / `mist` | Atmospheric haze | Mystery, depth separation |
| `smoke` | Drifting opacity | Drama, obscured vision |
| `rain` | Falling droplets | Mood, cleansing |
| `snow` | Falling flakes | Calm, cold, purity |

### Light Phenomena

| Term | Visual | Best For |
|------|--------|----------|
| `lens flare` | Light artifacts from source | Organic, warmth |
| `god rays` / `crepuscular rays` | Beams through atmosphere | Spiritual, dramatic |
| `caustics` | Light refracted through water/glass | Underwater, aquatic |
| `bokeh` | Out-of-focus light circles | Aesthetic blur |

## Color and Grade

### Color Temperature

| Term | Kelvin | Character |
|------|--------|-----------|
| `warm` | Low K (2000-4000) | Orange, amber, cozy |
| `neutral` | Mid K (5000-6000) | Daylight, balanced |
| `cool` | High K (7000+) | Blue, clinical, cold |

### Tonal Range

| Term | Meaning | Best For |
|------|---------|----------|
| `high contrast` | Deep blacks, bright whites | Drama, impact |
| `low contrast` | Lifted blacks, muted highlights | Soft, dreamy |
| `crushed blacks` | Very dark shadows | Cinematic, moody |
| `lifted blacks` | Visible shadow detail | Film emulation |
| `desaturated` | Reduced color intensity | Serious, documentary |

### Color Harmony

| Term | Meaning | Best For |
|------|---------|----------|
| `complementary` | Opposite colors (blue/orange) | Visual pop |
| `analogous` | Adjacent colors | Harmony, cohesion |
| `monochromatic` | Single hue variations | Unified, focused |
| `split toning` | Different colors in shadows/highlights | Cinematic grade |

## Quick Reference Card

**Building a prompt**:

```
[Camera movement] + [lens/DOF if relevant]
[Subject with material specificity]
[Action in present continuous]
[Environment with time/light context]
[Atmosphere and color direction]
[Special flags: seamless loop, locked camera]
```

**Example assembly**:
```
Slow dolly forward, shallow depth of field,
through forest of translucent crystalline structures,
particles drifting upward,
deep twilight blue hour, soft ambient glow from within forms,
desaturated cool palette with warm accent highlights,
seamless loop, locked camera
```
